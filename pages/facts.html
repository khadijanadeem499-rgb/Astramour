The Sun

More than 1.3 million Earths could fit inside the Sun. It makes up 99.86% of the solar system's mass!

#SolarSystem

The Moon

The Moon is moving away from Earth at 3.8 cm per year. In 50 billion years, it will take 47 days to orbit Earth!

#Earth

Venus Day

A day on Venus is longer than its year. It takes 243 Earth days to rotate but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.

#Venus

Saturn's Rings

Saturn's rings are only about 30 feet thick, despite being 175,000 miles wide!

#Saturn

Space Water

There's a massive water vapor cloud in space containing 140 trillion times the water in Earth's oceans.

#Universe

Diamond Planet

55 Cancri e, a planet 40 light-years away, is thought to be composed largely of diamond!

#Exoplanet

Coldest Place

The coldest place in the universe is the Boomerang Nebula at -272°C (just 1°C above absolute zero).

#Nebula

Hottest Place

The hottest known place is the core of a dying star, reaching 100 billion degrees Celsius!

#Stars

Milky Way

Our galaxy contains 100-400 billion stars and is 100,000 light-years across.

#Galaxy
